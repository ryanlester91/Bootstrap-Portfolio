About Me

Hi! My name is Ryan Lester. I have lived most of my life in Colonial Heights, Virginia and attended James Madison University in Harrisonburg. My list of hobbies are still expanding; I am practicing guitar and chess, enjoy reading fiction and nonfiction books, and am hoping to get back into hiking as the weather warms up.

My brother has inspired me to explore the tech industry so I am currently training in full-stack web development. Looking forward to learn about the back-end of the stack. Coding will open a much larger world, as I go from mere passive Internet browsing to building, manipulating, and creating on the medium.
